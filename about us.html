About Totesphere Partenerships

We are Totesphere Partenerships, Nigeria's pioneer social enterprise organization.

Our Mission:

Is to change the world by providing support to businesses making a profit of less than 50,000 naira a day so they can expand their business without reliance on high-interest loans.

Our Vision:

Is to empower micro-businesses to break the cycle of poverty and thrive, we envision a world where entrepreneurs earning less than $10 a day have access to the resources and support they need to grow and succeed.

Core Values:

1. Empowerment: Enhancing the capacity of Nigerian businesses and communities.
2. Transparency: Ensuring accountability and clarity in our grant-making and donation processes.
3. Inclusivity: Fostering equal opportunities for all, regardless of background or location.
4. Innovation: Encouraging creative solutions to Nigeria's economic and social challenges. "We do not invest to exact profit, we invest to build wealth".
5. Collaboration: Building partenerships with stakeholders to amplify impact.
6. Social responsibility: We aim to setup businesses that would sustain our social commitments, we won't be reliant on grants and donations.

Partnerships:

Organizations, including government agencies, NGOs, and private sector companies.
