Donation FAQs

What types of donations do you accept?

We accept monetary donations via credit card, PayPal, or check.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, all donations are tax-deductible to the fullest extent allowed by law.

Can I make a recurring donation?

Yes, you can set up a monthly or annual recurring donation.

How do I know my donation is secure?

Our site uses SSL encryption and secure payment processing.

What is your refund policy?

Donations are non-refundable, but can be directed to a different program.

Grant FAQs

What types of grants do you offer?

We offer grants for business development.

Who is eligible for grants?

Non-profit organizations, schools, and community groups.

How do I apply for a grant?

Submit an online application with proposal, budget, and supporting documents.

What is the grant review process?

Applications are reviewed by our grants committee within 6-8 weeks.

How are grant recipients selected?

Based on alignment with our mission, impact, and financial need.

General FAQs

What is your mission?

How do you use donated funds?

Funds support grants, programs, and operational costs.

Can I volunteer?

Yes, contact us for volunteer opportunities.

How do I contact your organization?

Email, phone, or mail; see contact page.

Do you have a privacy policy?

Yes, see our website's privacy policy page.
