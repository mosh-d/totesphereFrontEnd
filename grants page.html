Empowering Lives, Growing Communities

Our grant program is dedicated to driving positive change in Nigeria by supporting individuals and initiatives that address critical challenges and foster sustainable growth. We provide financial assistance through various grant categories:

Eligibility Criteria

  • Be an individual, organization, or business aligned with the purpose of the chosen grant category.
  • Demonstrate a clear need for financial assistance.
  • Provide a detailed and viable plan for the use of funds.
  • Commit to contributing to the growth and well-being of their local communities or sectors.

How to Apply

Please note: Only fully completed forms will be reviewed and considered.

Grant Online Application Requirements and Guidelines:

1. Check Your Eligibility: Review the requirements for your chosen grant category.
2. Complete the Application Form: Provide all required details and documentation.
3. Submit a Proposal: Share a clear and impact proposal that outlines your objectives and how the grant will be used.
